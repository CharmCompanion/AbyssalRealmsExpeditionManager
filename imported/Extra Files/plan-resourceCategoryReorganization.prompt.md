### Key Mechanics from Design Documents

**From FantasyCityBuilder_StatsSchema.csv:**
- Population: Total citizens, worker availability, employed/unemployed tracking
- Housing: Evolution levels based on goods/services, desirability scores
- Goods: Resource stocks, food supply variety, distribution coverage
- Services: Coverage for fire, health, culture, religion
- Economy: Treasury, income, expenses, trade balance, tax impact
- Trade: World map, trade partners, route status, favor levels
- Military: Unit count, morale, resource readiness

**From Mechanics chat.txt (Pharaoh/Zeus Analysis):**
- Housing evolves based on: food variety + consumer goods + services + desirability
- Workforce: Houses supply workers; industries need proximity to housing
- Supply chain: Raw resources ‚Üí Production ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Distribution ‚Üí Consumption
- Goods requirements: Common housing needs food, basic goods; elite needs luxury goods
- Culture/entertainment: Affects housing evolution and morale
- God favor/reputation: Affects blessings, curses, trade, diplomacy
